1987年9月起进入华东师大计算机科学系学习，并於1991年7月获工学学士学位，1999年7月和2002年7月在该校获得教育学（高等教育学）硕士学位和教育学（课程教学论）博士学位。现任教育部人文社会科学重点研究基地课程与教学研究所研究员丶上海数字化教育装备工程技术研究中心主任，兼任教育部教育信息化专家委员会秘书长，教育部高等学校教育技术专业教学指导委员会副主任委员，教育部高中信息技术课程标准修订专家组组长。研究方向为教育技术学丶学习科学丶课程与教学论和教师教育。

演讲题目 从常态应用到深度融合——基於大陆四省中学生的调查看教育信息化建设
演讲内容简介 信息技术被认为正在革命性地影响并改变着当前中国的教育；然而对大陆东西部四省的高中生调查表明目前信息技术在我国高中实际教学过程中应用程度有待提高。我们认为信息技术与学习者/教师丶知识丶外部体制机制一同构成了教与学的生态系统，而作为後来者的信息技术与其所在生态系统的契合度不高是造成当下信息技术与教育教学不能深度融合的主要原因；只有将信息技术有机地融入这个生态丶在教与学的“常态”中发挥作用，才能真正对教育教学产生持续影响。

Title:

Developing 21st learning skills: What we are doing?

Abstract:

It is not easy to take education truly into the 21st century although we are already living in it. The development of 21st century learning skills have received increased attention in recent education reforms, especially by enabling innovative learning methods that integrate the use of technologies, inquiry and problem-based approaches, and community practices. In this talk, I shall discuss some related projects being carried out by Harvard Graduate School of Education, Mazur’s Group at Harvard University, and the KM&EL Lab of the University of Hong Kong. The discussion will be based on my recent visit and collaborative work at Harvard, with a focus on learning in challenging situations.

Bio:

Dr. Maggie Minhong Wang is an Associate Professor at the University of Hong Kong, and Director of the KM&EL Lab. She has visited Harvard University, MIT, and University of Cambridge as a visiting scholar in recent years. Her research interests include technology-enhanced learning, knowledge visualization, workplace learning and higher education, business process management, and artificial intelligence. Her research has been published in Educational Research Review, Computers & Education, Medical Education Online, Internet and Higher Education, Educational Technology & Society, Information & Management, IEEE Transactions on Education, Knowledge-based Systems, Journal of Knowledge Management, among others. She is the Editor of Knowledge Management & E-Learning and Associate Editor of Information Management. More details can be found at http://web3.edu.hku.hk/magwang/.
