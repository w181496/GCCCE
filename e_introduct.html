The 19th Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education (GCCCE 2015) will be held in Taipei, Taiwan on May 25-29, 2015. The conference is an annual international conference organized by the Global Chinese Society for Computers in Education (GCSCE). GCCCE conference has been developed as a major international event that gathers together researchers and educators around the world who are interested in Information Communication Technology (ICT) in education. The conference includes a lively mix of keynotes, workshops, submitted paper sessions and posters sessions. GCCCE 2015 invites participation from educational experts, policy makers, researchers, teachers, and educators in area of computer education application.
