GCCCE 2015 Call for Papers

 

Conference: The Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education(GCCCE) 2015

Sub-conference 1: Science of Learning, Computer Supported Collaborative Learning, and Artificial Intelligence in Education

Conference date: 25 (Monday) - 29 (Friday) May 2015

Conference venue: National Central University, Tai Pei

 

Rising from the reflection of cognitive science disciplines, research methods and ideas about learning method, the science of learning began to mature in the l990s and started to stand out as an independent discipline. It aims at carrying out cross-cutting empirical inquires of phenomenon of learning in authentic situations and understanding how learning happens with or without assistive technology. Meanwhile, the implement of computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education emphasis on making full use of the affordances of technology and the research result of the science of learning to design more effective individual and collaborative learning environment. As the next step of research, by studying students’ learning in innovation environment integrated by science and technology to direct and optimize the previous design of learning environment, over and over again, helping each other forward.

This sub-conference involves a wide range of subject areas, such as cognitive science, educational psychology, computer science, anthropology, social sciences, information science, cognitive neuroscience, instructional design and so on. In this sub-conference, we hope to bring together Chinese scholars and practitioners in this area from all over the world to exchange their thoughts and contributions.

Excellent paper will be recommended to JCE (Journal of Computers in Education, www.springer.com/40692) published by Springer.

 
The scope of papers will cover but not be limited to:

·Theories discussion of science of learning, computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·Activities design of learning science, computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·Instructional strategies and application of learning science, computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·Innovative practices of learning science, computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·Development of computer supported collaborative systems and tools.

·Evaluation of the effectiveness of computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·Obstacles and countermeasures of computer supported collaborative learning and artificial intelligence in education.

·The new learning model and new media literacy.

Note: we also welcome new topics besides the above-mentioned

Paper submission:

Authors should prepare submissions either in English or in Chinese (Long paper: 8 pages; Short paper: 4 pages; Poster: 2 pages). Submissions written in Chinese should include the title, abstract and keywords written in both Chinese and English.

Authors should submit papers in the Microsoft Word format. Please make use of the paper template provided on the conference website for preparing submissions.

Authors should make submissions by uploading papers onto the Submission System of the conference.

 

Potential journal publication

Submissions to this sub-conference will be recommended to publish in Global Chinese Journal for Computers in Education (in Chinese) (http://www.gcjce.net),  International Journal on Digital Learning Technology (in Chinese) (http://www.ijdlt.org), Journal of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences (in Chinese) (http://jlass.ntust.edu.tw/index.php/lass) and Journal of Computers in Education (in English).
