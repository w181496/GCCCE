GCCCE 2015 Call for Papers

 

Conference: The Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education(GCCCE) 2015

Sub-conference 8: Digital Technology, Innovation, and Education

Conference date: 25 (Monday) - 29 (Friday) May 2015

Conference venue: National Central University, Tai Pei

 

The Innovation of technological development brings forth a number of changes with education involved. There is the growing impact of E-learning trends on education in the future, including fields such as teaching, research, student learning, extending to family as well as  pre-school, basic education, higher education, vocational education and lifelong learning.

When E-learning combined with education, the most concern is how to enhance learning performance and boost motivation. Another issue needed to be addressed is how to collaboratively work together among teachers, students, teaching contents and activities and to provide students an innovative learning mode with E-learning accessibility. 

The sub-conference gathers researchers and educators around the global Chinese society for papers, presentations and publications to collaboratively initiate an area of technology in application to education for the purpose of accelerating E-learning researches to enter into the realm of a new digital century. The conference cordially invite participation from domestic and international experts in related field along with young scholars to disseminate and share ideas for research in the field of E-learning.

Paper submission:

Authors should prepare submissions either in English or in Chinese (Long paper: 8 pages; Short paper: 4 pages; Poster: 2 pages). Submissions written in Chinese should include the title, abstract and keywords written in both Chinese and English.

Authors should submit papers in the Microsoft Word format. Please make use of the paper template provided on the conference website for preparing submissions.

Authors should make submissions by uploading papers onto the Submission System of the conference.

 

Potential journal publication

Submissions to this sub-conference will be recommended to publish in Global Chinese Journal for Computers in Education (in Chinese) (http://www.gcjce.net),  International Journal on Digital Learning Technology (in Chinese) (http://www.ijdlt.org), Journal of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences (in Chinese) (http://jlass.ntust.edu.tw/index.php/lass) and Journal of Computers in Education (in English).
