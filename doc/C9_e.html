GCCCE 2015

(Call for Papers)

 

Conference: The Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education(GCCCE) 2015

Sub-conference 9: K-12 Teacher Forum

Conference date: 25 (Monday) - 29 (Friday) May 2015

Conference venue: National Central University, Tai Pei

 

The purpose of this session is to offer teachers and administrators of K-12 educational institutes for sharing their practical approaches and reflection of ICT into teaching and learning, ICT plan for K-12 schools, and educational technology leaderships. The following topics are valuable for this session, including digital reading, digital writing, digital story-telling, APP, cloud computing and digital game applied on teaching and learning.

The scope of papers will cover but not be limited to:

·ICT integration into teaching in creative approaches

·Policies of e-learning and mobile learning

·Effective practices of e-learning and mobile learning

·Effective teaching approaches of computer curriculum

·Effective teaching approaches of computer ethics

·Creative design of digital content

Paper submission:

Authors should prepare submissions either in English or in Chinese (Long paper: 8 pages; Short paper: 4 pages; Poster: 2 pages). Submissions written in Chinese should include the title, abstract and keywords written in both Chinese and English.

Authors should submit papers in the Microsoft Word format. Please make use of the paper template provided on the conference website for preparing submissions.

Authors should make submissions by uploading papers onto the Submission System of the conference.

 

Potential journal publication

Submissions to this sub-conference will be recommended to publish in Global Chinese Journal for Computers in Education (in Chinese) (http://www.gcjce.net),  International Journal on Digital Learning Technology (in Chinese) (http://www.ijdlt.org), Journal of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences (in Chinese) (http://jlass.ntust.edu.tw/index.php/lass) and Journal of Computers in Education (in English).
