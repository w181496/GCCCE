Call for Proposals

for Workshop of GCCCE 2015

Conference: The Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education(GCCCE) 2015

Conference date: 25 (Monday) - 29 (Friday) May 2015

Conference venue: National Central University, Tai Pei

 

The workshop aims to provide participants an academic communication platform, focusing on discussing and communicating topics about specific application of professional computer education, and further strengthening cohesion of GCCCE community researching specific topics. Workshop will be held in May 25, 2015 and May 26, 2015. Every workshop activity lasts 3 hours or 6 hours, including mini-conference, paper publishing, and the subsequent interaction and discussion. Looking forward to proposals in fields of computer education application related. Each chairman of the workshop will be responsible for the collection and assessment of the workshop papers, and the arrangement of workshop rules.

Workshop proposals should include:

·Workshop Theme

·Keywords of workshop and papers

·Narrative of workshop theme connotation (within 1000 words)

·“Call for Papers” notice draft

·Names, service organization, email, and brief introduction of workshop chair, co-chair

·Workshop Committee (about 3 to 5 persons)

Important Dates:

Workshop Proposal Submission Deadline: January 17, 2015

Workshop Proposal Acceptance Notification: January 24, 2015

Workshop Paper Submission Deadline: March 28, 2015 (4 pages for short paper, 8 pages for long paper)

Workshop Paper Acceptance Notification: April 4, 2015

Workshop Paper Finalized Deadline: April 10, 2015

Workshop Authors Registration Deadline: April 15, 2015
