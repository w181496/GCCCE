GCCCE 2015

(Call for Papers)

 

Conference: The Global Chinese Conference on Computers in Education(GCCCE) 2015

Sub-conference 6: Technology Enhanced Language Learning

Conference date: 25 (Monday) - 29 (Friday) May 2015

Conference venue: National Central University, Tai Pei

 

In response to the global Chinese demand for research diversity, GCCCE 2015 on Technology Enhanced Language Learning (TELL) (C5) offers the opportunities for the exchange of state-of-the-arts research and development in the related field. TELL contributes to the advancement of first, second and foreign language learning with the aid of Information and Communication Technology. The main objective of the sub-conference is to stimulate dialogues among TELL scholars and practitioners so as to gain insights into and synergy of the theories, pedagogies, technological design and evaluation of TELL environments.

 

The scope of papers will cover but not be limited to:

·Theoretical foundations and perspectives of TELL

·Technologies and applications for TELL

·Learning strategy and learning styles for TELL

·Development of digital contents for TELL

·Technology-assisted language learning assessments and evaluations

·Socio-cultural perspectives related to TELL

·Teacher professional development for school-based deployment of TELL

·TELL in open, distance, flexible, blended and seamless learning contexts

·TELL for informal learning (such as, in libraries, careers, and social network)

 

Paper submission:

Authors should prepare submissions either in English or in Chinese (Long paper: 8 pages; Short paper: 4 pages; Poster: 2 pages). Submissions written in Chinese should include the title, abstract and keywords written in both Chinese and English.

Authors should submit papers in the Microsoft Word format. Please make use of the paper template provided on the conference website for preparing submissions.

Authors should make submissions by uploading papers onto the Submission System of the conference.

 

Potential journal publication

Submissions to this sub-conference will be recommended to publish in Global Chinese Journal for Computers in Education (in Chinese) (http://www.gcjce.net),  International Journal on Digital Learning Technology (in Chinese) (http://www.ijdlt.org), Journal of Liberal Arts and Social Sciences (in Chinese) (http://jlass.ntust.edu.tw/index.php/lass) and Journal of Computers in Education (in English).
